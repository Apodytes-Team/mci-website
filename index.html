About Market-Creating Innovations

The programme aims to train young (16-21 years old) innovators in skills needed to implement market-creating innovations, which will eventually boost the development and prosperity in their communities.

Solutions Rooted in Marketing and Innovation

  • 1

    What is MCI?

    Market-creating Innovations targets nonconsumption by transforming complicated and expensive products into products that are simple and affrodable, making them accessible to a whole new segment of people. Market-creating innovators are system builders who establish new markets, create value and enable their communities to flourish.

  • 2

    Why MCI for Youth?

    • Young people are the ones most at risk of unemployment.
    • Instead of only focusing on equipping youth with the necessary labour market skills, our efforst should equally focus on enabling young people to create new market opportunities for their peers.
    • Youth entrepreneurship education (where available) is limited to basic business skills rather than helping students become system thinkers and empowering them to create and capture value and seek business opportunities where others don't.

Agenda

Week of Online Training

July 18th - July 22nd

During this first week, participants will receive extensive training on the topics related to entrepreneurship, market-creating innovation, technology and sustainable development, provided worl renown experts.

8 Week Project Work

July 25th - October 31st

In the next 7 weeks, participants will be working on their ideas for technology-driven market-creating innovation that is addressing a non-consumption segment in their country or region. They will have scheduled sessions per week with mentors and trainers.

5 Day In-Person Finals

TBC

The top 25 global innovators will be invited to take part in the final stage of the programme - A 5 day bootcamp held in-person. It will take place at two different locations - ABB in Zurich and during the WEF annual meeting in Davos. The teams will get the chance to show their projects and work with experts to improve on them.
